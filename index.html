How a project runs

Four stages, in order

Every engagement follows the same sequence, whether it's a single retrofit or a full structural package.

01

Site assessment

We document existing conditions and gather the constraints that will shape the design.

02

Structural analysis

Load modelling and code-compliance review against the relevant Indian Standard codes.

03

Design development

Drawings, specifications, and a calculations package ready for permitting and tender.

04

Construction support

Site visits and submittal review through to close-out, so the build matches the drawing.

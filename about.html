About Meridian

Engineering that shows its work.

Twelve years of structural and architectural practice, built on full calculations and site visits — not just a stamped sheet.

Our story

Started by three engineers and one shared complaint

Meridian Structures was founded in 2012 by three structural engineers who kept running into the same problem: clients received a stamped drawing set and little else — no explanation of how the numbers were reached, and no one to call when a contractor asked a question on site.

We built the practice around the opposite habit. Every project leaves our office with the full calculation package alongside the drawings, and the engineer who ran the numbers stays reachable through construction. Most of our work today comes from architects and contractors we've worked with before.

We're based in Bhopal and take on projects across Madhya Pradesh, with occasional work further afield when the project calls for it.

How we work

Four habits we don't skip

  • 01

    Show the math

    You receive the full calculation package, not only the stamped drawing. If a contractor or authority asks why, the answer is already in your hands.

  • 02

    Visit the site before we model it

    A soil report tells you part of the story. An engineer walks the site before any load calculation begins.

  • 03

    One coordinated set, not two competing ones

    Architectural and structural drawings are checked against each other before they reach your contractor.

  • 04

    Stay reachable through construction

    The engineer who designed it answers the RFIs. Questions on site get answered by someone who already knows the building.
